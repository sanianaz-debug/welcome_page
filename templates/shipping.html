Shipping / Delivery Policy

Fuvani Studio provides AI-generated interior design transformations for your rooms. All services and outputs are delivered digitally through our website and/or email.

1. Digital Delivery Only

We do not ship any physical products. All room transformations, design previews, and related files are delivered online. There are no courier charges, packaging fees, or physical shipping involved.

2. Delivery Time

After a successful payment and upload of your room image(s), Fuvani usually generates your AI design transformations within a few minutes.

In some situations (high demand, maintenance, or technical issues), delivery may take up to 24 hours. If there is an unusual delay, we will try to inform you on the website or by email.

3. How You Receive Your Designs

  • Your transformations are made available inside your Fuvani Studio account/dashboard on our website.
  • In some cases, we may also send a secure link to your registered email address.
  • You can view, download, and save your AI-generated images from there.

4. Incorrect Email or Access Issues

If you entered a wrong email at the time of signup or payment, or you are unable to access your designs, please contact us with your payment details so we can help locate your order and update your information.

5. No Physical Shipping

Since Fuvani Studio only delivers digital designs, there is no concept of physical delivery, logistics tracking, or “shipping status”. Your order is considered delivered once the designs are available in your account or sent to your email.

6. Contact

For any delivery-related questions or access issues, you can reach us at support@fuvani.com or through the form on our Contact page.
